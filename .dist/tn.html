Tamil Nadu Tourist Destinations

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is a beautiful hill station located in the Western Ghats of Tamil Nadu. Known for its pleasant climate, scenic landscapes, and famous spots like Coaker's Walk and Kodai Lake, it’s a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Ooty

Ooty, often referred to as the 'Queen of Hill Stations,' is one of the most popular tourist spots in Tamil Nadu. With its vast tea gardens, serene lakes, and a pleasant climate, Ooty is ideal for a relaxed getaway. Don’t miss a ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway!

Mahabalipuram

Mahabalipuram, also known as Mamallapuram, is a historic town famous for its ancient rock-cut temples and intricately carved stone monuments. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for the Shore Temple, Pancha Rathas, and the Arjuna's Penance bas-relief.
