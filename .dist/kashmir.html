Kashmir Tourist Destinations

Srinagar

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, is famous for its picturesque landscapes, serene Dal Lake, and beautiful houseboats. Visitors can enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake, explore the Mughal Gardens, and shop for authentic Kashmiri handicrafts.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg, often referred to as the 'Meadow of Flowers,' is a popular hill station and skiing destination. With its lush green meadows, snow-capped mountains, and the famous Gulmarg Gondola, it offers a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility for tourists.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam is a beautiful town located on the banks of the Lidder River. Known for its scenic beauty, it serves as a base for the Amarnath Yatra. Visitors can enjoy trekking, fishing, and horse riding, or simply relax amidst its lush pine forests and tranquil surroundings.
