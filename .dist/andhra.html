Tourist Attractions in Andhra Pradesh

Araku Valley

Araku Valley, located in the Eastern Ghats, is known for its beautiful landscape, coffee plantations, and tribal culture. It offers a peaceful retreat surrounded by hills and lush greenery, making it a popular destination for nature lovers.

Vishakhapatnam

Vishakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a coastal city with beautiful beaches, scenic hills, and historical landmarks. It is a major tourist destination for its natural beauty and vibrant culture, including the famous Ramakrishna Beach and Kailasagiri Hill.

Gandikota

Gandikota, often called the 'Grand Canyon of India,' is located on the banks of the Pennar River. Known for its stunning gorge and historic fort, Gandikota is a hidden gem in Andhra Pradesh, offering breathtaking views and a glimpse into India's history.
